Essential Baby Care Tips

;

Nadeesha Rajapaksha

20.02.2022

Bringing a new life into the world is a beautiful and transformative experience, but it also comes with its fair share of challenges and uncertainties. As a new parent, taking care of your precious bundle of joy requires knowledge, patience, and a whole lot of love. To help you navigate the early days of parenthood, we've compiled a list of essential baby care tips that will set you on the path to confident and effective parenting.

;

1. Create a Safe Sleep Environment

Safe sleep is paramount for your baby's well-being. When putting your baby down to sleep: Place your baby on their back to reduce the risk of Sudden Infant Death Syndrome (SIDS). Use a firm and flat mattress in a crib or bassinet designed for babies. Remove soft bedding, toys, and pillows from the sleep area. Keep the room at a comfortable temperature (around 68-72°F or 20-22°C).

;

2. Feed Your Baby Responsively

;

Whether you're breastfeeding or formula feeding, responsive feeding helps establish a strong parent-baby bond: Feed your baby on demand, looking for hunger cues like rooting, sucking, or putting their hands to their mouth. Ensure a comfortable and quiet feeding environment. Practice skin-to-skin contact during feeding to promote bonding.
